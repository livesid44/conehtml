Regulatory Compliance

US Regulatory Standards – Contact Center Audit Reference

S.No Regulation / Standard (US) What it Covers (Contact Center Impact) Agent Must-Do Actions (Operational)
1 GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act) + Safeguards Rule
Privacy & Data Security 		Protect customer info; maintain security controls
  • Authenticate before disclosure
  • Share minimum necessary information
  • Follow secure channels
  • Never disclose to unverified callers
2 PCI DSS
Telephone Payments Guidance 		Secure handling of card data over calls; call recordings risk
  • Don't store CVV
  • Avoid repeating SSN, TIN
  • Keep card details out of notes and recordings
3 Reg Z (Truth in Lending) – Billing Error Resolution
FCBA Mechanism 		Credit-card billing error disputes (FCBA mechanism)
  • Log dispute correctly
  • Provide billing error address/channel
  • Capture required details
  • Set expectations on timelines
  • Avoid collection pressure on disputed amount per policy
4 Fair Credit Billing Act (FCBA)
Consumer Dispute Rights 		Consumer dispute rights and creditor duties
  • Advise consumer on written dispute process only when required
  • Ensure acknowledgment and investigation workflow is triggered
5 UDAAP (Dodd-Frank)
Unfair / Deceptive / Abusive 		Prohibits unfair/deceptive/abusive conduct
  • Use approved scripts
  • Don't misrepresent fees/Annual percentage rate/outcomes
  • Give clear disclosures
  • Document accurately
6 FDCPA / Regulation F
Collections 		Debt collection communication limits (time/place/attorney/third parties)
  • Call only in permitted hours
  • No workplace calls if prohibited
  • No third-party disclosure
  • Stop/route if attorney represented
7 OFAC / Sanctions (FFIEC BSA/AML)
Sanctions Compliance 		Sanctions compliance program expectations
  • Don't tip off
  • Escalate hits
  • Document
  • Follow "block/reject/hold" procedure per compliance
8 FCRA Adverse Action Notice
Credit Report Decisions 		Required notices when decisions based on credit reports
  • Route to notice workflow
  • Explain CRA rights
  • Do not provide unauthorized "reasons"
  • Keep to approved language
